# 3D Printing Notes

## Hardware Fit

All 3D printed parts were designed with:

- **3.4 mm diameter clearance holes** for M3 screws
- **6 × 6 × 2.5 mm cavities** for M3 DIN 562 square nuts

## Default Print Settings

Unless otherwise noted, parts were printed with:

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Material | Bambu PLA Basic |
| Printer | Bambu P1S |
| Nozzle diameter | 0.4 mm |
| Preset | `0.20 mm Standard @BBL X1C` |
| Supports | Not required when properly oriented |

## High-Temperature Part

The `Center_HighTemp` body within the `Tube stabilizer` component was printed by the Northwestern Rapid Prototyping Lab on Formlabs printers using High Temp Resin.

This material was selected because it has a heat deflection temperature of **238 °C at 0.45 MPa**.
